What is Tigacoin?

TigaCoin is our answer to poachers and dormant commercial conservation organizations for endangered species. The token is tied to the Binance smart chain and will act as a link between caring investors and a specially designed gaming platform. Our team, after consulting with tigers, of course, decided to contribute to the protection and conservation of endangered species on the planet.
By supporting our coin, you not only become the owner of a valuable financial asset that will bring you profit in the future due to the technology of burning emission. In addition to this, you support every surviving tiger that roams the land. We are not a charitable organization, but our strategy involves a clear distribution of the commission from the purchase of cryptocurrency, some of which will go directly to the tigers.
Invited analysts and digital currency experts are already talking about our growth prospects and the possibility of raising the coin's value to almost the same level as the life of endangered tigers.
